    Rules

    Make sure not to ruin other people’s fun. You can attack people but don’t be malicious.
    Don’t hack in a way that causes server issues. (Xray fine, flyhacks arent)
    Don’t use the faithful texture pack
    Don’t threaten or harass members out of game or discord server.
    You may be kicked off the discord server if you talk more than you play.

    Gameplay Tweaks

    40% of players need to be sleeping to skip night
    You spawn within a 300 block radius of the world spawn point
    Players drop heads when killed by a player
    Bundles can be crafted
    Rotten Flesh can be smelted into Leather
